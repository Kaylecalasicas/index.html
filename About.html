About BDO

Corporate Profile

BDO is a leading full-service universal bank in the Philippines, offering a wide range of financial products and services. With over 1,700 branches and more than 4,700 ATMs nationwide, BDO is committed to providing convenient access to banking services for its customers.

Corporate Mission & Vision

Mission: To be the preferred bank in every market we serve.

Vision: To empower customers to achieve their goals and aspirations through innovative banking solutions and exceptional customer service.

Core Values

  • Commitment to Customers: Delivering superior value and service.
  • Commitment to a Dynamic and Efficient Organization: Encouraging flexibility, innovation, and continuous improvement.
  • Commitment to Employees: Fostering an environment of growth, excellence, and teamwork.
  • Commitment to Shareholders: Providing superior long-term returns.
